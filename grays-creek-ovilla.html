Community Overview

A Spacious Residential
Community in Ovilla

Gray's Creek Ranch Estates – Ovilla is planned as a residential community centered around large homesites, thoughtful design, and a quiet North Texas lifestyle. The project is positioned for buyers who want more space without losing access to nearby cities, schools, shopping, and employment centers.

Market Position

Positioned in a Growing
Residential Corridor

As growth continues south of Dallas, communities like Ovilla are attracting buyers who want more land, privacy, and a higher-quality residential lifestyle. The demand for acreage homesites continues to support thoughtfully planned communities across Ellis County.

📈

DFW Southward Growth

The Dallas–Fort Worth metropolitan area continues expanding southward, bringing infrastructure, employment, and residential demand to Ellis County.

🌾

Demand for Acreage Living

Post-pandemic lifestyle shifts have sustained strong buyer interest in larger lots, more space, and lower-density communities across North Texas.

🛣️

Strong Regional Connectivity

Convenient access to major roadways including US-287 and I-35E connects Ovilla residents to employment and amenity centers across the region.

🏡

Limited Supply of Large-Lot Communities

Quality one-acre communities in established areas like Ovilla remain limited, supporting sustained buyer interest and long-term community value.

⭐

Attractive Lifestyle Market

Ovilla offers the lifestyle appeal of a smaller community — trees, space, quiet streets — with proximity to all major DFW amenities.

🔒

Long-Term Community Value

Established communities with larger lots and thoughtful planning tend to hold long-term residential appeal and value over time.
