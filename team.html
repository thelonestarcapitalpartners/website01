The People Behind the Projects

Daniel Almaguer III
Founder & Managing Partner

Daniel Almaguer III is a Texas real estate developer focused on land acquisition, residential development, construction coordination, and project execution across the Dallas–Fort Worth market. He leads the Gray's Creek developments with a focus on creating high-quality communities, disciplined execution, and long-term value.

Kirk Kibler
Co-Founder & Head of Operations

Kirk Kibler brings decades of construction and operational leadership experience to the team. As the former owner of WBK Construction, he has overseen large-scale commercial and residential projects throughout Texas. At Gray's Creek, Kirk supports construction management, operations, scheduling, budgeting, and execution.

Hilario Ortiz
Principal Engineer

Hilario Ortiz is a professional engineer with experience in land development, infrastructure design, civil planning, and municipal coordination. Through HCE Engineering, he supports the Gray's Creek projects with engineering oversight, infrastructure planning, and technical development guidance.

Elsie Thurman
Strategic Advisor – Zoning & Entitlements

Elsie Thurman is a zoning and permitting specialist with extensive experience working with municipalities, planning teams, and development groups across North Texas. She supports the team with land use strategy, entitlement guidance, and development planning.

Shahab Smoqi
Strategic Development Advisor

Shahab Smoqi brings a background in business transformation, systems thinking, technology, and strategic growth. He supports the team with market research, business analysis, investor communication materials, process improvement, and long-term growth strategy.
